Ng Conf Honduras

Ng Conf Honduras is the first conference of Angular at Honduras, where national and international people will attend to share ideas, knowledge, experiences and create new connections.

We decided to organize NG Honduras because we are passionate about the Angular community, we have all those benefits, so we feel we have the right to be able to give back a little bit of everything that the community has given us. We want that this conference to be as accessible as a possible, so that people from all over the world can come and enjoy the event.
